Hiragana contains 42 characters.

# Vowels
There are 5 vowels in Hiragana:
* あ - A (Think apple)
* い - I (Think of two i's)
* う - U/ew (Think of a boxer getting punched in stomach and the noise it would make)
* え - E (Think of an energetic Ninja)
* お - Oh (Think of a UFO)
